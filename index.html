What good looks like

Simple journey One question at a time, large buttons, no clutter, easy to use on a phone.
Approved wording Keep content tightly aligned to the agreed process and avoid adding interpretation.
Clear end state Every route should end in a specific instruction, escalation, or next action.

What to avoid

Too much text Do not bury the decision in long paragraphs.
Mixed messages Do not show conflicting instructions or optional wording when the process is mandatory.
Dead ends Do not leave the user without a clear next step.
